IDEA BEHIND PARK&BARK

Welcome to Park&Bark, where we it is all about you and you furry best friend! Our busy lifestyle can get pretty lonely pretty quick so having an app where you can connect with other dog owners can be really helpful. As a dog owner myself, i realised there is no website that is keeping track of state and conditions of dog parks in Zagreb and thought that had to change. Also considering finding friends irl can often be hard I created Tindog, a fun "dating app" for dogs that gives you a chance to make new friends for yourself and your pet.

DOG PARKS

You can use our Parks feature to rate and comment on your local dog parks yourself! You can simply click on a park of your choice and see comments from other users regarding its condition. You can also "check in" into a park so that users can see you are at that park at that moment. Looking at list of other users in the park can also help you awoid crowds if your dog doesn't do well in noisy environment.

PARK&BARK BOX

We all love to spoil our pets and what better way to spoil them than to have montly subscription box full of toys, treats and equipment for them. For the ptice of 49,99 euros a month you are guaranteed at least 80 euros worth of content. We carefully select all items keeping in mind your dogs health and trying to maximize their joy.

TINDOG

Explore Tindog and find your dog friends to play with or go on adventures together. If you want a chance meet the dog on a card and its owner click heart button. If the owner of the dog liked your dog card too it will show you that it is a match. If it is a match you can send that owner an email and get yourdo ready for a play date.
